Harry Potter and the Sacred Text is an audio podcast founded by Vanessa Zoltan and Casper Ter Kuile,

and hosted by Vanessa Zoltan and Matt Potts, in which the Harry Potter books are read as a sacred text. Each episode, the characters and context of the one chapter in the Harry Potter series are explored through a different central theme like 'vulnerability', 'betrayal', or 'friendship'. The podcast, which charted #2 on the US iTunes Charts a few months after its inception in 2016,[1] has been described as "Bible studies for J.K. Rowling fans".
